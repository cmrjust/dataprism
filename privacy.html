Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

GLOBAL PRIVACY COMPLIANCE STATEMENT: DataPrism Research Network strictly complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and international digital user confidentiality acts. This policy discloses how we handle, distribute, and protect your personal identification records during public statistical sampling panels.

1. Information Collection and Categorization

To operate our market research system effectively, DataPrism gathers two primary streams of intelligence:

  • Voluntary Account Metadata: Email addresses, preferred authentication credentials, internal identity references, and custom transactional details generated by users during active operations.
  • Automated Telemetry Data: Public internet protocol (IP) metrics, system configurations, browser identifiers, active canvas resolutions, operating system tags, and precise referral track timestamps.

2. Methods and Frameworks of Data Processing

DataPrism limits the utilization of your personal data strictly to operational validation parameters:

We deploy advanced, automated security filters to scan telemetry records for systemic risk profiles (including proxy networks, duplicate registration trails, and scripted inputs). Personal profiles are entirely segmented from business matrices; all commercial research submissions are compiled into mass aggregated groups and verified using anonymized batch keys. Individual responses are never routed to third-party ad brokers outside of these global demographic groups.

3. Cookies, Tracking Tags, and Localized Storage

Our infrastructure deploys tracking cookies and browser data objects to maintain persistence for secure active sessions, analyze anti-fraud benchmarks, and prevent single users from launching multiple dashboard tasks. You maintain the authority to completely block web tracking storage architectures through localized browser security tools, though doing so might cause script failures across our survey routing nodes.

4. Third-Party Frameworks and Partner Distribution

DataPrism partners with certified upstream consumer research sponsors (such as survey wall adapters and institutional advertising networks). While checking security metrics, our network may share encrypted, anonymized hardware tags with corporate checking APIs to verify your location and connections. These third-party networks follow their own unique privacy standards, and we encourage you to inspect their structural documentation directly.

5. International User Rights (GDPR & CCPA Frameworks)

Regardless of your geographic region, DataPrism grants all platform members extensive control over their database records. You maintain the absolute right to:

  • Request comprehensive digital reporting regarding the categories of personal metrics we retain.
  • Request fast correction of incorrect or outdated profile details.
  • Request the complete deletion of your registration index and account records from our live databases.

To exercise any of these privacy rights, you can file an official digital query to our support queue at cs@dataprism.eu.cc. We will complete all verified database actions within statutory deadlines.

6. Data Security and Encrypted Safeguards

We deploy industrial-grade data encryption, administrative access restrictions, and strict server firewalls to protect against unauthorized data exposure or malicious network manipulation. However, no digital transmission channel can be guaranteed as entirely immune to external exploits; users maintain all final transmission risks.
